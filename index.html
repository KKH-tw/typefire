ABOUT TYPEFIRE

What is TYPEFIRE?

TYPEFIRE is a minimalist typing speed test designed for people who want to improve their words-per-minute (WPM) and accuracy. It's fast, distraction-free, and works on any device.

How it works

Choose a duration — 15, 30, or 60 seconds — then start typing. Words are drawn from a curated library of science, philosophy, and general knowledge passages, so every session feels fresh.

  • Green characters = correctly typed
  • Red characters = mistakes
  • Press Space to advance to the next word

Leaderboard

After each round, enter a nickname to submit your score to the global leaderboard. Compete with players around the world and see where you rank.

Sharing

Tap the SHARE button on the results screen to generate a score card image you can share on social media or send to friends.

Tips to improve

  • Focus on accuracy first — speed follows naturally
  • Keep your eyes on the word display, not your fingers
  • Practice daily, even just one 30-second session
  • Try the 60s mode to build stamina
PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

TYPEFIRE ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and handle information when you use TYPEFIRE.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to operate the service:

  • Leaderboard scores: If you choose to submit a score, we store your chosen nickname, WPM score, accuracy percentage, and submission timestamp in our database (Firebase Realtime Database by Google).
  • Usage data: We may use Google Analytics to collect anonymised data about how visitors interact with TYPEFIRE, including pages visited and session duration. No personally identifiable information is collected.

Cookies and Advertising

TYPEFIRE may display advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

For more information on how Google uses data, please visit Google's Privacy & Terms.

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google): used for leaderboard data storage. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Wikipedia API: used to fetch typing passages. No personal data is sent.
  • Google Fonts: used to load fonts. Google Privacy Policy

Data Retention

Leaderboard entries are retained indefinitely unless you request removal. To request deletion of a leaderboard entry you submitted, contact us (see below).

Children's Privacy

TYPEFIRE is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
